# User Manual

LeadGenius AI helps you go from business context to lead generation and outreach in one flow.

## 1. First-Time Setup

1. Open the app.
2. Click API Settings (top-right).
3. Paste your Gemini API key.
4. Click Save Key.

Alternative: set NEXT_PUBLIC_GEMINI_API_KEY in .env.local.

## 2. Business Profile

Complete your business inputs:

- Company Name
- Industry
- Targeted Industries
- Services/Products
- USP
- Target Market
- Geographic Target
- Budget Level
- Sales Goal and Currency
- Preferred Outreach Channel
- Competitors (optional)

Tip: Use Magic Auto-Fill to describe your business in plain language and auto-populate form fields.

## 3. Generate Strategy

Click Analyze Business and Generate Strategy.

Output includes:

- Business model analysis
- Best industries and profitable niche direction
- Expected close rate strategy
- 30-day action plan
- 90-day scaling strategy

## 4. Lead Generation

Open Lead Generation tab and click Generate Leads.

What you can do:

- View lead cards with classification (Hot/Warm/Cold)
- Filter by classification and sales angle
- Change lead status (New, Contacted, Qualified, or custom)
- Delete specific leads
- Load more leads
- Export all leads as CSV
- Review source links collected from web/maps grounding

## 5. Outreach Templates

In Outreach Templates tab:

- Generate general outreach templates
- Generate lead-specific templates from each lead card
- Copy templates to clipboard
- Clear templates when needed

Templates include:

- Initial outreach message
- Day-2 follow-up
- Day-5 final follow-up

## 6. Final Strategy Report

Go to Final Report tab and click Generate Report.

The report summarizes:

- Lead totals and breakdown
- Opportunity level
- Expected conversion insights
- Revenue potential framing
- Final recommendations

## 7. Best Practices

- Fill profile details with specific, real business context.
- Keep targeted industries focused for better lead quality.
- Generate outreach after reviewing lead sales angles.
- Track lead status consistently before export.
- Re-run lead generation periodically for fresh opportunities.

## 8. Known Behaviors

- If no API key is found, the app opens Settings automatically.
- API key is stored in your browser localStorage when set in app.
- If Gemini quota is exceeded, you may see temporary rate limit messages.
